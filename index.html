Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about home physiotherapy in Noida

What is home physiotherapy and how does it work?+
Home physiotherapy is a service where a licensed physiotherapist visits your home to provide treatment. With PhysiQo, you simply message us on WhatsApp with your location and preferred time, we match you with a verified physio nearby, and they visit your home for a session — no travel needed.
How do I book a physiotherapist at home in Noida?+
Booking is easy. Just message PhysiQo on WhatsApp at +91-Will bw Avaialable soon. Share your location, issue, and preferred time slot. We will confirm a verified physiotherapist for your home visit within minutes — no app needed.
Are the physiotherapists verified and licensed?+
Yes. Every physiotherapist on PhysiQo is background-checked, holds a valid BPT or MPT degree, and has verified clinical experience before they are onboarded. Sessions are also rated after every visit, and poor performers are removed from the platform.
What conditions can PhysiQo physiotherapists treat?+
PhysiQo physiotherapists treat back pain, neck pain, knee pain, shoulder pain, sports injuries, post-surgical rehabilitation (knee, hip, spine), sciatica, frozen shoulder, arthritis, neurological conditions, stroke rehab, and elderly mobility & fall prevention.
Do I need to download an app to use PhysiQo?+
No. PhysiQo works entirely on WhatsApp. There is no app to download. You book sessions, track your recovery, and communicate with your physio all through WhatsApp — which you already use every day.
How much does a home physiotherapy session cost in Noida?+
Session pricing varies based on the type of treatment and session duration. Message us on WhatsApp for a transparent, no-hidden-fee quote tailored to your condition and location in Noida.
Can I book physiotherapy for a family member?+
Absolutely. You can book PhysiQo sessions for anyone in your family — parents, spouse, or children — all through your WhatsApp. Just let us know who the appointment is for and their condition.
Which areas in Noida does PhysiQo serve?+
PhysiQo currently serves all major sectors and localities across Noida, Uttar Pradesh — including Sector 18, 22, 44, 50, 62, 63, 76, 100, 110, 120, 128, 135, 137, 150, and more. Message us on WhatsApp to confirm availability in your specific area.
