Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 2026

1. Request Account Deletion

At PhysiQo, we respect your privacy and your right to delete your personal information. If you wish to permanently delete your PhysiQo account and associated personal data, you can submit a deletion request.

2. How to Request Deletion

You can request deletion using any of the following methods:

  • Email us at support@physiqo.in
  • Send a WhatsApp message from your registered mobile number.
  • Contact our customer support through the PhysiQo website.

3. Information Required

Please include:

  • Your full name
  • Registered mobile number
  • Email address (if applicable)
  • Reason for deletion (optional)

4. What Happens After Your Request

  • Your identity will be verified.
  • Your PhysiQo account will be permanently deleted.
  • Your personal information will be removed from our active systems.
  • Some records may be retained if required by applicable laws or for dispute resolution.

5. Processing Time

Most deletion requests are completed within 7 business days.

6. Data That May Be Retained

Certain transaction records, invoices, or legally required information may be retained for compliance with Indian laws. These records are not used for marketing purposes.

7. Contact Us

For any questions regarding data deletion, contact us at:

Email: support@physiqo.in
Website: https://physiqo.in
