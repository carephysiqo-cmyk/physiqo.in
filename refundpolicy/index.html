Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 2025

1. Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellation: Cancel at least 2 hours before your scheduled session — no charge.
  • Late cancellation: Cancellations within 2 hours of the session may incur a 50% cancellation fee.
  • No-show: If you are not available at the scheduled time and location, the full session fee may be charged.

2. Refund Policy

  • If PhysiQo or the physiotherapist cancels your session, a full refund will be issued.
  • If the session quality was unsatisfactory due to a verified issue, we will offer a free re-session or refund at our discretion.
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

3. How to Cancel

To cancel a session, message us on WhatsApp at least 2 hours before the scheduled time. Mention your booking details and reason for cancellation.

4. Disputes

For any payment disputes or refund requests, contact us at contact@physiqo.in. We aim to resolve all disputes within 48 hours.
